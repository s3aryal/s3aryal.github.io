UI/UX Designer

Softweb Solutions is a Chicago based tech consulting firm that provides state-of-the-art solutions to make your business better. Our areas of expertise can be divided into three core areas: Enterprise Mobility, Internet of Things, Web Application Development and Cloud Consulting.

At Softweb Solutions, we are looking for a creative, passionate individual to join our Design and UIUX team. We need someone who is excited about new technology, has a fresh perspective on design and trends in the digital experience age, and is willing to learn new tools and software to keep up with UX/CX and market research in all businesses and industries. Ideally for this role, you are a designer that is multi-faceted, talented and eager to learn new things, and has a keen sense of the overarching experiences, customer journeys, and how applying empathy to experiences can improve design.

For this role, we are looking for a UIUX Designer to fill these requirements. Below are the essential skills for this position:

  • Ability to understand complex business goals, product & technical content, and Interaction Design in order to create engaging & effective experiences for our customers
  • Ability to research, create and present product user journey maps, user task flows, interaction models, storyboards
  • Can develop new approaches to complex design problems and convey these designs in the form of presentations, prototypes, and design specifications
  • Can collaborate on user experience planning with a lead experience architect
  • Has a strong conceptualization ability, strong visual communication ability, drawing skills and sketchbook technique
  • Has experience with user interface design patterns and standard UCD methodologies
  • Has strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Has experience with a variety of design tools such as Photoshop, Illustrator, Fireworks, Visio, and Dreamweaver
  • Experience with Invisionapp, Smaply, Sketch, and Adobe XD is a plus.
  • Experience with Telerik UI, Bootstrap and HTML5 is also a plus.

Education

  • Bachelor’s or above.
