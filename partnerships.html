Partnerships

At Alchemy, we believe that our ExoShield technology can benefit a wide array of markets and market segments. We’re constantly looking for partners to help us serve our existing markets and scale to new markets. Here are a few ideas of the kinds of partners we’re looking for.

Installation

Leverage your existing retail locations and boost your revenues by offering low overhead ExoShield installation services to your customers.

Distribution

If you have well-developed channels and expertise in aftermarket product distribution, ExoShield might be a great add-on to your existing products.

Dealership

Increase your revenues by offering ExoShield as an upgrade option with each vehicle you sell, and install ExoShield in-house to maximize your margins.

Insurance

Reduce glass claims by offering ExoShield to your policy holders at a discounted cost and boost loyalty by keeping their windshields frost free.
