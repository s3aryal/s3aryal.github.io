Three Pillars of Data Analytics

Data Analytics is an amalgamation of data science, data visualization and data engineering.

  • DATA ENGINEERING
    UNLOCK THE BUSINESS VALUE OUT OF YOUR BIG DATA

    Data Engineering includes data infrastructure and architecture. It is all about data ingestion, data processing and search & retrieval.

    At Softweb, our data engineers gather and collect all the data, store it and then do batch processing or real-time processing on it. We use the latest tools and technologies like Hadoop, Apache Spark and Mongo DB.

  • DATA SCIENCE
    UNLOCKING AND OPERATIONALIZING DATA INSIGHTS

    Data Science helps you realize the full potential of your existing data using the knowledge of statistics, machine learning, and data mining tools.

    At Softweb, we help you achieve your goals by analyzing your historical data and extracting actionable insights from it. Our data science services will help you identify new revenue opportunities, make informed decisions, get accurate predictions and stay ahead of competitors.

  • DATA VISUALIZATION
    WE CAPTURE AND PROCESS DATA TO PROVIDE YOU WITH EASY-TO-UNDERSTAND VISUALS

    The volume of data your business generates every day is soaring high. In such a scenario, it is not just important to get the valuable information out of it, but also to understand the possibilities of what you can do out of that data.

    At Softweb, our data visualization experts use latest tools and unique techniques to capture data, process it and display it in various visuals that can be seen on all the platforms and devices. We help you fetch insights, accelerate decision-making and leverage opportunities to minimize the cost and risks.
