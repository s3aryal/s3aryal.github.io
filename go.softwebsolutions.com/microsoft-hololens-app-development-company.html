Here are just some of the examples how mixed reality
(the merging of real and virtual worlds to create new visualizations and experiences)
is transforming industries:

Construction, engineering and architecture

Though many digital prototyping, designing, and modeling software are available, it has been quite difficult for engineers and designers to show the dynamics and visualize 3D. Hololens apps make it easy for people to deal with designs, concepts and structure, facilitating quick decision-making. A holographic representation provides an unmatched level of real-world proportion, scale, form, and perspective, which brings transparency across the entire of a construction project.

Learn more

Manufacturing and product development

HoloLens apps can help improve collaboration between different team across the entire product development process by allowing team members to interact in real-time and be on the same page at all times. The mixed reality enables faster prototyping, 3D model visualization, and faster and more confident decision-making.

Learn more

Healthcare

With HoloLens, doctors can create a simulated environment to get a virtual yet realistic experience of performing complex surgeries, which can take the medication to a whole new level. Not just medical practitioners, but also biomedical engineers can make the most of HoloLens to analyze and solve medicinal and biological problems.

Learn more

Education

Mixed reality will not only help capture the attention of students, but also enable professors to explain the most complex things in the most effective way. For example, 3D models of human organs will make it very easy for students to gain a better understanding of the concepts they are studying.

Learn more

Science

Scientists can learn at a faster pace than ever before with a holographic experience that blends a view of the real world with computer-generated visuals. Mixed reality enables scientists to accelerate future exploration and provide new capabilities to them when conducting critical science experiments.
