About Event

IoT is already upon us and is disrupting every industry out there. So, what exactly is IoT? Simply put, the Internet of Things is a collection of devices and objects that are embedded with processors and sensors, which can connect with each other over the Internet in order to send and receive data.

Softweb Solutions, COMPAREX, and Microsoft have joined hands to bring this IoT event to you. With scalable cloud platforms, higher wireless connectivity, and cheaper microprocessors, implementations of IoT are gearing up. During the event, we will review how IoT can be enabled, considering its hardware, software, and licensing perspective. Our goal with this informative event is to let business decision makers know how IoT can help in taking their organization forward.
