About Software Gurus

Software Gurus, originally founded in Waterloo is a software consulting company with the knowledge and wisdom of a large cap publicly traded company, but with the drive of a start up. With our knowledge and experience, we are able to complete projects from start to finish with the utmost efficiency and care.

At Software Gurus, our ambition and vision is quite simple: to deliver the best projects, as efficiently as possible. We believe that with the right platform technologies, a culture that encourages creative risk taking, and a modern approach towards creating and developing technology, we can make much better software solutions than anyone else out there.
